Token-based User Authentication

By: Paz Davila, Spencer Kittredge, Jesus Garcia
This is Group 13's ECE413 Project. In this project the user will be able to create and account to record their heartbeat and blood oxygen level. The information will be stored using token authentication. Users can view their information and changed all aspects of it besdies their email. They can have multiple devices stored in their account and view their graphs showing information about their heartbeat and blood oxygen level. Finally they can change how frequent the particle device asks for readings and from what time of the day the device is allowed to ask for information.
